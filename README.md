## 👨‍💻 About Me

I'm a **Data Scientist and AI Engineer** from Pakistan, passionate about turning data into decisions and research into real products. I specialise in building end-to-end intelligent systems — from raw data pipelines to deployed machine learning models and Generative AI applications.

With **39 verified certifications** from IBM, DeepLearning.AI, Stanford, Google, Coursera, and DataCamp, I back every skill with structured learning and hands-on projects. My current obsession is **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)** — building AI that actually knows what it's talking about.
